Practical Information

Accomodation

For those looking for a place to stay during the event, here are some suggestions for hotels and hostels, depending on your budget and preferences.

Under 500 SEK per night

  • STF af Chapman & Skeppsholmen - Bed in dorm: from 290 SEK per night. This place is right next door to the wedding venue for those who fancy a short walk home (30 seconds)

  • City Backpackers Hostel - Bed in dorm: from 280 SEK per night. Centrally located hostel in a nice old building with a sauna and a bar
  • Skanstulls Hostel - Bed in dorm: from 235 SEK per night. Located on Södermalm, the hipster part of Stockholm. Free coffe/tea!

500 to 700 SEK per night

  • Old Town Lodge - “Sleeping booth” from 640 SEK per night. Sleep in a basement vault in a 300 year old building in Stockholm’s Old Town
  • Mosebacke Hostel - Single Room: from 625 SEK per night. Located on Södermalm, the hipster part of Stockholm. Breakfast included
  • Birka Hostel Single Room: from 673 per night. Centrally located, yet on a quiet street.

700 and up

  • STF af Chapman & Skeppsholmen - Single room: from 715 SEK per night. This place is right next door to the wedding venue for those who fancy a short walk home (30 seconds)
  • Pensionat Odengatan - Double Room: from 899 SEK per night. A small lodging with few rooms and rave reviews.
  • Hotell Gyllene Geten - Double room: from 900 SEK per night. In the middle of Old Town, 100 m from the royal castle.
